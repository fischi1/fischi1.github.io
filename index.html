About me

I live in Vienna, Austria and have graduated with a bachelor's degree in Media Informatics and Visual Computing at the Technical University of Vienna in October 2018.

My interest in coding started very early thanks to my fascination for video games. This also led me to choose a HTL for information technology and a university program that also features computer graphics. In my free time I spend a lot of time with Unity or something CG related. 

I'm interested in coding in general, computer graphics, game programming and game engines. This is also the reason for the topics of my matriculation and bachelor theses.
